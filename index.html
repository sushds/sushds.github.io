Research & Writing

Are Scope 1/2 emissions disclosures in Dutch ESG equity funds verifiable? — A systematic ESRS E1 data quality assessment

Cross-validating company-reported Scope 1 and 2 emissions data from Dutch ESG equity fund holdings against EU ETS verified data from the European Union Transaction Log (EUTL). The research question: are ESRS E1 disclosures — as they currently stand — comparable, internally consistent, and independently verifiable at a fund portfolio level? The pipeline is open-source and reproducible, using public CDP, EUTL, and fund holdings data.

In progress — designed for submission to SSRN and presentation at PyData Amsterdam.
